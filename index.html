About us

Enhancing Business Performance since 2004

Established in 2004, we began as a specialist Business Intelligence (BI) consultancy. It was established with the purpose of providing tailored business services and turnkey IT solutions to the South African private and public sectors.

Kakanyo has grown and diversified into other complementary areas including Business Process Management (BPM), Programme and Project Management and end-to-end ICT software and infrastructure services and solutions.

With a solid foundation and innovative recent acquisitions that shape its bright future, the company maintains being a good corporate citizen and is gaining authority as a thought leader of Data-driven Business.

Sales Performance

We provide solution to enhance your sales performance by using Customer Intelligence to generate actionable insights and develop a data-driven sales strategy that is implemented by combining/developing innovative technology solutions to digitize and automate the strategy for efficient ROI.

Governance, Performance, Risk & Compliance

GRC has an immense effect on performance. Our solutions offer a single platform to enable a sustainable, efficient, and effective GRC program aligned with strategy and performance.
